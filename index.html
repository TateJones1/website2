This elegant little pokemon is a new Generation 9 starter, along with Quaxly and Fuecoco. However, this one is has something to offer that others don't. CUTENESS!!!!

It evolves into Floragato at level 16, then into Meowscarada at level 36. Making it a dark and grass type after the final evolution.

In my humble opinion, I really think this is the best Cat pokemon. Only because it looks identical to my cat.
